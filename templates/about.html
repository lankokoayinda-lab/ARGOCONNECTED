About Agroconnect+

Connecting farmers, buyers, and processors across Cameroon with simple digital tools.

Our Mission

We exist to empower Cameroonian farmers with fair access to markets, affordable equipment rentals, and trusted processor relationships. Agroconnect+ makes agriculture more efficient, transparent, and profitable for local communities.

Our Story

Founded in 2026, Agroconnect+ grew from a vision to remove barriers in the agricultural supply chain. By linking farmers directly with buyers and processors, we help reduce waste, increase income, and support sustainable farming growth.

Why Farmers Choose Us

Trusted Network

Access local buyers, processors, and lenders through a platform built for agriculture.

Affordable Access

Rent tools instead of buying them, and manage your farm expenses with more flexibility.

Easy Growth

Get support from a community that understands your needs and helps you grow.
